Sweet Girl Bakeries was opened in August 2020, a year after Uyinene Mrwetyana passed away from gender-based violence.We then took it apon ourselves to use our baking gifts to lift up our community and spread awareness about gbv.There are 40% of woman in South Africa who expereince sexual assault in thier lifetime and that is why 40% of our proceedings will go towards woman shelters in South Africa.

Our vision is to expand our bakeries across South Africa so that in doing so we willbe able to help more and more womans shelters in South Africa.
